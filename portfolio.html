Role : Full Stack Developer

Technology used : MEAN, Bootstrap4, html5, css3 and heroku

Demo Link : click here

So what is this project about ?.. And what did I do ??..

Skoll is a product for aspiring parents, Who want their kids to get admission in standard schools in and around their city. Over a span of two months I have built this product from scratch i.e.,front to back single handedly by myself.

Skool initially authenticates user with their registered username and password and allows them to search for the schools with criteria. The user can apply to schools from one click of the button by filling the form. Once the application is completed the application gets saved in mlabs remote server. School with admin key can access the applications and shortlist them based on their criteria. The admin functionality is also added into the application.
